10 useful features of regular training


Exercise is any type of physical activity that makes your muscles work, and above your body, burn extra calories.

There are many different types of physical exercises - swimming, various types of running, walking, dancing, etc.
Regular exercise has been proven to help improve mood and reduce feelings of depression, anxiety and stress.

During exercise, it affects the areas of the brain that are responsible for creating feelings of anxiety and stress. The sensitivity of the brain to serotonin and norepinephrine, which relieve depression, is also increased. During exercise, the production of endorphins is also stimulated, which stimulate positive emotions and reduce susceptibility to pain.

2. Exercise can help get rid of excess weight. Many studies confirm that physical inactivity is one of the main factors in the development of obesity. In order to understand how physical exercises contribute to weight loss, it is necessary to trace the relationship between physical activity and energy expenditure.

3. Exercise is good for your muscles and bones.

An interesting fact is that sports such as gymnastics, running, soccer, basketball provide the formation of bones of a higher density than when swimming or cycling.
